    Harrison Family Website Privacy Policy

    Last updated: November 6, 2025 · Contact: info [@] harrisonfamily [D0T] us

    1. Introduction

    The Harrison Family Website (“we,” “our,” “us”) provides a private digital archive of family history and media. This Privacy Policy explains how we handle personal information, including data processed through Google Sign-In, in compliance with Google’s API Services User Data Policy and applicable privacy laws.

    2. Scope

    This policy applies to all use of the website located at https://harrisonfamily.us and its subpages (the “Site”). It specifically governs:

    • Authentication via Google Identity Services.
    • Access to private genealogical content derived from the Gramps family database.
    • Any data hosted in our AWS infrastructure (S3 + CloudFront) and served through Cloudflare.

    3. Data We Collect

    3.1 From Google Sign-In

    • Your verified Google account email address.
    • Your Google profile name and ID token, for identity verification.

    We do not request or access any other Google account data (e.g., contacts, photos, Drive files).

    3.2 From Site Interaction

    • Access logs (browser type, IP address, timestamp) collected by AWS CloudFront for diagnostic and security purposes.
    • No tracking cookies or analytics services are used.

    3.3 From Genealogical Data

    • Family data derived from a read-only export of Gramps records, including names, relationships, dates, places, and media.
    • Living persons’ details are visible only to authenticated and whitelisted users.
    • No additional personal data is collected from you when you browse family pages.

    4. How We Use Your Data

    Data Type Purpose Retention
    Google account email Verify identity and confirm whitelisted access to private content. Not stored permanently; verified in-session only via token validation.
    Google ID token Authenticate session locally in your browser (no server-side storage). Discarded upon sign-out or page close.
    Access logs Detect abuse, ensure system stability. Rotated automatically by AWS (approximately 90 days).
    Gramps genealogical data Display family history. Stored in static JSON on AWS S3; updated manually by archivists.

    5. Data Sharing and Disclosure

    We do not share, sell, or transmit user data to any third parties. The only external systems involved are:

    • Google Identity Services, which handles sign-in and token issuance.
    • Amazon Web Services (AWS), which stores static site and data assets.
    • Cloudflare, which provides DNS and HTTPS routing.

    Each provider processes minimal data necessary for website functionality under its own privacy policy.

    6. Security

    • HTTPS enforced for all traffic via Cloudflare + AWS ACM certificates.
    • All data hosted on private S3 buckets using Origin Access Control (OAC).
    • JSON and media served through CloudFront with restricted access policies.
    • Living-person data visible only after client-side token validation using Google Sign-In.
    • MFA required for administrative AWS access.
    • ETL scripts operate in read-only mode when accessing the live Gramps database.

    No sensitive data (e.g., tokens, credentials) is stored in public repositories.

    7. Data Retention and Deletion

    • Authentication data is stored only in your browser session.
    • Access logs expire automatically after approximately 90 days.
    • Family data persists indefinitely for archival purposes but can be updated or removed by authorized archivists upon request.

    To request deletion or correction of personal information, email info [@] harrisonfamily [D0T] us.

    8. Children’s Privacy

    This site is intended for family members and is not directed to children under 13. Accounts for minors are managed by their guardians.

    9. International Data Transfers

    All data is stored and processed in the United States using AWS (us-west-2 region).

    10. Your Rights

    You may request any of the following:

    • Confirmation of whether your data appears in our genealogical exports.
    • Correction or removal of inaccuracies about you or your immediate relatives.
    • Removal of media in which you appear.

    Send requests to info [@] harrisonfamily [D0T] us with sufficient identifying information.

    11. Changes to This Policy

    We may update this Privacy Policy periodically. The most recent revision date appears at the top of this page. Material changes will be announced on the Site before taking effect.

    12. Contact

    If you have privacy concerns or requests regarding your data, email info [@] harrisonfamily [D0T] us with the subject line “Privacy Inquiry — Harrison Family Website.”
